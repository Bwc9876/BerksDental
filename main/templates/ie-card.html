We're Sorry To Inform You, Our Website No Longer Supports Your Browser (Internet Explorer).

Browsers We Recommend:

  • Google Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (macOS Only)

Why don't we support this browser?

The company that made Internet Explorer (IE), Microsoft, made an announcement that they will no longer be supporting the browser. They announced that as of August 17, 2021 they will not allow internet services with IE. As such we have decided it was best not to do the extra work of supporting a browser that will be shut down before the end of the year. More information can be found in the links below.
